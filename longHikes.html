Long hikes

These are the most extreme of all of our extreme hikes, and are usually two weeks or longer. They include trekking, local culture and food, as well as nature excursions. These are our physically demanding hikes, so if you are planning to do one of these you should be in reasonably good physical shape!

Quick facts about long hikes packages

  • Average guided tour group size: 10 to 20 guests
  • Price range: starts at 2000$ per person and go up to 3400$ range.
  • What's included: transport, visa permit, accommodations, meals (number of meals vary by tour), tour excursions, leaders and guides
  • Primary audience: solo travelers , couples, groups. Guest age tend to range from 25 to 35 years of age.
  • Destinations: Australia, Argentina, Chile, India .
