Camping hikes

Camping hikes are longer than short hikes and include 3 or more nights camping. You may bring your own camping equipment, or we can supply it for you.

Quick facts about camping hike packages

  • Average guided tour group size: 8 to 16 guests
  • Price range: starts at 1000$ per person and go up to 2400$ range.
  • What's included: transport, visa permit, accommodations, meals (number of meals vary by tour), tour excursions, leaders and guides
  • Primary audience: solo travelers , couples, groups. Guest age tend to range from 24 to 35 years of age.
  • Destinations: Morocco, Mongolia, USA (Wyoming & Montana state) .
