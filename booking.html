Why travel with Adventure Trail Hikes

Local connections

Adventure Trails works closely with local organizations to ensure that much of the profit goes back to the local communities. This may be in the form of infrastructure and trail maintenance to support local businesses and communities.

Respect for nature

Many of our hikes include extra activities such as bird watching and short courses on edible local plants.

Culture

In the interest of cultural exchange and sustainability Adventure Trails also includes cultural activities, which include experiences of various local customs, dress, theatre, dance, and music.
